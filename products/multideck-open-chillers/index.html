Multideck Open Chillers

Our Multideck Open Chillers (also known as Dairy & Deli Cases or Air Curtain Merchandisers) are designed for refrigerated product display in supermarkets, grocery stores, convenience stores, and retail chains. The open-front design allows customers to access products easily while improving product visibility and encouraging impulse purchases.

Suitable for beverages, dairy products, fresh food, packaged meals, fruits, and vegetables, these chillers maintain stable temperatures between 2°C and 8°C. Advanced air cooling technology creates a consistent cold air curtain to help preserve product freshness throughout the display area. High-quality painted steel panels and thick insulation improve durability and energy efficiency.

Equipped with imported Sanyo or Panasonic compressors, copper evaporators, and digital temperature controllers, these units deliver reliable performance in demanding retail environments. Both plug-in and remote refrigeration systems are available. Remote units help keep compressor heat outside the store, improving customer comfort and reducing indoor heat buildup.

Multideck Open Chiller

Multideck open chillers are widely used in modern retail environments because they combine product visibility with convenient customer access. Unlike cabinets with doors, open-front displays allow shoppers to pick up products quickly, helping improve the overall shopping experience.

These units are commonly found in supermarkets, convenience stores, grocery chains, fresh food markets, and retail food outlets. They are often used for dairy products, beverages, packaged foods, salads, fruits, vegetables, and ready-to-eat meals. Their vertical display structure helps retailers maximize product exposure while making efficient use of floor space.

One of the most common questions from distributors is how an open cabinet can maintain proper refrigeration. The answer lies in the air curtain system. High-speed cold airflow forms an invisible barrier across the front opening. This helps keep cold air inside the cabinet while maintaining product temperatures within the recommended range.

Another important topic is the choice between built-in and remote refrigeration systems. Built-in units are easier to install and are often preferred for smaller stores. Remote systems place the condensing unit outside the sales area, reducing heat and noise inside the store. This solution is especially popular in larger supermarkets and warm climate regions.

Many buyers also ask about energy consumption. Factors such as insulation quality, compressor efficiency, airflow design, and store temperature all affect performance. High-quality compressors and proper cabinet design help improve cooling efficiency and reduce operating costs over time.

Product arrangement is another key consideration. Open displays perform best when products are stocked correctly and airflow paths remain unobstructed. Proper merchandising helps maintain temperature consistency while improving product presentation.

In many international markets, these cabinets are also referred to as Supermarket Wall Chillers because they are commonly installed along store walls to create continuous refrigerated display sections. Some distributors use the term Open Chillers when discussing self-service refrigeration equipment with easy customer access. Fresh produce retailers may also classify certain models as Vegetable Chillers when they are used for displaying fruits, vegetables, and packaged fresh products.

For wholesalers and supermarket equipment suppliers, multideck open chillers remain one of the most important product categories. They help retailers increase product visibility, improve shopping convenience, and create an attractive refrigerated merchandising area that supports daily sales growth.
