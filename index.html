Certifications

CE

The CE mark is a mandatory requirement for products sold in the EEA, confirming compliance with EU safety, health, and environmental regulations to allow free circulation within the market.

IEC

The IEC certification indicates that a product complies with international standards for electrical and electronic safety, performance, and quality, facilitating its acceptance in global markets.

MSDS

An MSDS (Safety Data Sheet) provides critical information regarding the properties, hazards, safe handling, and emergency procedures for chemical substances, ensuring workplace safety and regulatory compliance.

ISO-9001

ISO 9001 certification confirms that a company has implemented a quality management system that consistently meets customer and regulatory requirements while focusing on continuous improvement.
