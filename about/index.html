About Us

Qingdao Lihongda International Trade Co., Ltd. is a professional supplier of commercial freezers based in Shandong, China. We have our own factory. We manufacture various types of commercial freezers, including display freezers, meat refrigeration freezers, stainless steel refrigeration freezers, and more. We accept any private customization, including voltage, frequency, and material customization. All our products have passed CE testing and possess IEC test reports and IS09001 certification.

All our products come with a one-year warranty.

We have professional engineering support, an efficient sales team, and competitive pricing,which has attracted customers from all over the world. We export to more than 40 countries, including Europe, Poland, Serbia, Turkey, Russia, the United States, Mexico, Brazil, India, Thailand, the Middle East, and South Africa.

After several years of development, we have made significant progress and are equippedwith automated production lines. Our R&D department provides strong technical support, enabling us to take on OEM and ODM projects. We can produce around 200 units per day.

We are a professional commercial freezer manufacturer in China and look forward tolong-term cooperation with various businesses.
